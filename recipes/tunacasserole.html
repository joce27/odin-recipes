Tuna Casserole

Description

I've never had a tuna casserole but I would like to try it. It's got tuna and potato chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 package egg noodles
  • 2 cans condensed cream of mushrooom soup
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1 cup frozen green peas
  • 1/2 can sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup crushed potato chips

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil. Cook egg noodles at boil for 7-9 minutes; drain
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  3. Mix noodles, condensed soup, 1 cup cheese, tuna, peas, mushrooms, and onion
  4. Transfer mixture into a baking dish
  5. Top the mixure with crushed potato chips and remaining cheese
  6. Bake in oven for 15-20 minutes
