Chocolate Mousse

Description

I don't remember ever having mousse but I love chocolate so I would want to try this.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup Baileys Irish Cream
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch salt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Beat with electric mixer until stiff peaks form
  3. Chill until serving
