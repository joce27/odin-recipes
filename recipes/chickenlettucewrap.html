Chicken Lettuce Wrap

Description

This looks healthy and I remember lettuce wraps tasted good when I've had them before.

Ingredients

  • 1 roasted chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped water chestnuts
  • 1/3 cup Asian-style salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 head Boston lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Mix chicken, carrots, and water chestnuts
  2. Stir salad dressing and yogurt separately
  3. Pour mixed dressing over the chicken mixture
  4. Spoon chicken mixture into lettuce leaves
